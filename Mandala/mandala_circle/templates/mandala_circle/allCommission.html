Commissions

Yes, I accept commission requests!
A commissioned piece is a custom, unique, original artwork that is hand drawn and painted by me but made
exclusively for you. You are able to choose the size, colours and material you'd like the piece to be created on in
order to make it truly your own.

Pricing
A full quote will be given during our email correspondence based on the size and intricacy of the piece you'd like
me to create.
Commissions start at Rs5000 for a 20cm piece.

To discuss commissioning a piece, please either email me at mandalacircle@gmail.com or fill in the form below with
as much detail as possible regarding your vision for the artwork and I will get back to you at my earliest
convenience.
