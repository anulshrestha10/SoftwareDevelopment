About Me

I’m Manisha Basukala, I do a bunch of stuff that helps me in pursuing my passion for artistic creativity.Being an artist
isn’t just about putting pencil to paper, or brush to canvas. Art can and does mean so much more than that, to so many
people. Art is a way of seeing the world, a way of expression, oftentimes a compulsion: We simply must create!
Since the last seven years I’ve been making Mandala portraits in various dimensions and sizes. Mandala is basically
a form of drawing that represents the exemplary beauty of nature. We should learn to see everyday life as mandala - the
luminous fringes of experience which radiate spontaneously from the empty nature of our being. The aspects of our
mandala are the day-to-day objects of our life experience moving in the dance or play of the universe. Moreover, it is a
mediation-al form of art, which soothes both, the maker and the observer.
While pursing the passion for Mandalas, I’ve understood the vital role of colors, shape, textures. This realization keeps
on improving the vision that I bear toward my passion of contributing towards the great artistic fraternity. Apart from being
a Mandala artist, I am doing bachelor in IT under the name Bachelor of Information Technology from the Coventry Universy.
Mandala Circle Mandalas wall art and decor are designed for everyone and for every occasion. They are designed with a lot
of love and eye to detail. Once ready, they are checked for quality and packed with immense love, gratitude, all ready
to be shipped out. I wish my mandala wall arts bring you smiles and happiness!
