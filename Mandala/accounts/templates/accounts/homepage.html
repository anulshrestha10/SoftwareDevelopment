The Beauty of
Detail.

Life in Detail is a representation
of the intricacies of our world. It
is learning to appreciate the little
things around us and
understanding how by living day
to day we are contributing to our
own bigger pictures without
realising. Just as every little
detail in my artwork is
necessary to complete the
overall piece, each and every
day (no matter how bad)
is significant when fulfilling our
destinies.

The Meaning of
Mandala

The word mandala in Sanskrit is circle.
Mandala is a spiritual and ritual
symbol in Hinduism and Buddhism,
representing the universe.
The circular designs symbolizes
the idea that life
is never ending and
everything is connected. The
mandala also represents spiritual
journey within the individual
viewer.
